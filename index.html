About Me

I’m John Eiries DC. Nacu, fondly known as Nacs among friends and peers. As a passionate and dedicated athlete, I’ve immersed myself in the world of sports, excelling in basketball and track & field. Competing fuels my drive, but my love for sports goes beyond the court and the track. Staying fit and active is a lifestyle I wholeheartedly embrace, not just for the physical benefits but for the discipline, teamwork, and resilience it instills.

My athletic journey is a story of growth, perseverance, and the pursuit of excellence. I take immense pride in sharing my passion with others, whether it’s inspiring someone to take their first step toward fitness or encouraging them to push beyond their limits. Beyond personal achievements, I aim to foster a supportive community where the values of sportsmanship, hard work, and camaraderie shine through.

For me, sports are not just about winning but about learning, improving, and bringing out the best in myself and those around me. Whether on the court, the track, or in daily life, I strive to live by the principles that athletics has taught me and to share that enthusiasm with everyone I meet.
